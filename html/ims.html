About us

Our insurance company offers a complete range of coverage. The services include life insurance, vehicle insurance and property insurance,investment funds and products related to savings and deposits.

Our values

Stability

We have an established position on the insurance market. We pursue our activities in a responsible and safe manner.

Integrity

We use transparent rules and a simple language to communicate. We design transparent and custom-made products and keep our commitments in our activities.

Innovation

We actively seek improvements in our business operations. We use our client knowledge to design solutions enhancing their security and ability to take care of the future.

Responsibility

We keep our promises and help in difficult situations (not just by paying out indemnities or adjusting claims). Our goal is to provide education in finance, health and broadly understood security. We are also not indifferent to social problems. We watch threats and respond wherever we have a chance to change anything for the better.
